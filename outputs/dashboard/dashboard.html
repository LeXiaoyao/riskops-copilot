Jargon glossary · 一眼读懂术语

下列术语会反复出现在异常 / 归因 / 过程证据 / ROI 模块中，先看一眼避免迷失。
M1 D7 recovery rate
share of newly-overdue (M1) loan amount that gets any payment within day 7
M1 客户在逾期第 7 天前完成任意还款的金额占比，是贷后早期催收效果的核心指标。
PTP (promise to pay)
the borrower verbally / textually agrees to pay by a date; PTP keep rate = share that actually paid
客户口头或文字承诺还款日期；PTP 履约率衡量承诺后真实兑现比例。
Vendor / line
outsourced collection agency (vendor) and its operation queue (line)
委外催收机构（vendor）及其作业线路 / 队列（line）。
Recovery rate
share of overdue principal + interest recovered within a defined window
在指定窗口内已回收的逾期本息占应收逾期金额的比例。
AI outbound coverage
share of overdue cases reached by AI-driven outbound calls (demo only — no real customer contact)
AI 外呼覆盖到的逾期案件占比；本 demo 不产生真实外呼。
Driver / attribution
which slice (channel / region / customer segment / vendor) explains the change in the headline metric
解释整体指标变化的关键切片（渠道 / 区域 / 客群 / 供应商等）。
